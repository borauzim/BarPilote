# BarPilote : Design Prompts - Batch Client (Jean) 🍏🥤

**Style Global** : Apple "Consumer" Experience. Clarté absolue, menu ultra-rapide (PWA). Utilisation de belles photos de produits (générées). Typographie SF Pro Rounded pour une touche plus accueillante. Micro-interactions sur le bouton "Ajouter au plateau". Zéro friction.

## 📸 01. Entrée & Navigation (QR Scan)

### Prompt 041 : Splash Screen Client (PWA)
"Minimalist splash screen for BarPilote customer app. Pure white background. In center, a high-quality 3D render of a cold beer glass with condensation. Below, the 'BarPilote' logo in elegant SF Pro. Bottom label: 'Ready to fly. Please wait...'. Soft fade-in animation."

### Prompt 042 : Accueil Menu - Bienvenue au Lounge
"Main landing page after QR scan. Top header: 'Welcome to Lounge Pilote - Table 12'. Below, a scrolling horizontal bar of categories: 'Beers', 'Spirits', 'Softs', 'Wines'. Active category in bold blue. Minimalist search icon at top right. Aesthetic: Airy, spacious, premium."

### Prompt 043 : Grille de Produits (Menu Page)
"A grid of drink products for BarPilote. Each card: High-res square photo of the drink on a soft gray background. Name below (e.g., 'Heineken 33cl'), Price ('4,500 FC'). Top right of card: Small '+' icon in a circle. Clean, professional, Apple Store app vibe."
