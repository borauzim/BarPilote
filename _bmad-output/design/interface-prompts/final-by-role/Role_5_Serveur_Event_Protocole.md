# BarPilote : Design Prompts - Role 5 : Serveur d'Évènement (Protocole / Staff) 🕵️‍♀️🤵‍♂️💐

**Style Global** : Apple "Silent Service". Palette **Orange Vibrant & Anthracite**. Zéro mention de prix. Focus sur l'identification des invités, la discrétion et la gestion des "Boucles VIP" lors des cérémonies.

---

## 🔑 01. Mission & Accès (Protocol Check-in)

- **Prompt 069 : Vue Mission du Protocole**
  "Main task list for the protocol staff. 'Table 1: Serve 2nd round of Loop', 'Table 8: Refill soft drinks'. Simple checkmark buttons to confirm delivery. Focus on speed and discretion. Dark mode for evening events."
- **Prompt 032 : Interface "Protocole" - État du Stock Réservé**
  "Specialized UI for event staff. No prices shown. Progress bar for 'Champagne reserved for Table Honor'. '12/48 bottles used'. Status: 'Stock Safe'. Buttons: 'Add Drink (Invitation Only)'. Apple Sports app style metrics."
- **Prompt 311 : Toast "Mode Évènement Actif" (Micro-copy)**
  "Persistent banner: 'Wedding Mode: Prices are hidden'. Pure white bar at top, orange text."

---

## 🤵‍♂️ 02. Identification & Service VIP (Honor Guest)

- **Prompt 070 : Identification du Client VIP (Events)**
  "Client info screen for protocol. 'Table 1: Uncle of the Groom'. Order history for this specific guest. 'Preference: Whisky with 1 ice cube'. Apple Contacts style, very elegant and informative."
- **Prompt 071 : Commande "À la Plume" (Manual Entry for Protocol)**
  "Quick entry screen to note a special request from a guest. Selection grid of beverages. One-tap validation. No payment mention. Text: 'Compliments of the Host'. Soft blue validation button."
- **Prompt 151 : Rencontre Client & Confirmation (Handover)**
  "Handover screen. Tshala shows her phone to Jean. Large green/orange screen: 'Enjoy your flight!'. Jean sees his order reflected correctly. Success sound visualization (Orange wave)."

---

## 🔄 03. Gestion des Boucles VIP (Event Loops)

- **Prompt 072 : Configuration de la Boucle VIP**
  "Special setup for VIP tables. 'Serve 1 bottle of Spirit every 90 mins'. Control switch to 'Auto-approve' or 'Manual-dispatch'. Visual: A gold circular arrow icon. High-end Apple-style settings menu."
- **Prompt 073 : Vue d'ensemble des Boucles Actives**
  "A 'Clock View' showing all ongoing loops in the hall. Timers countdown until next delivery for each table. 'Table 3: 4 mins'. Color-coded urgency. Beautiful, data-dense but readable Apple design."

---

## 📱 04. Communication & Alertes (Field Feedback)

- **Prompt 076 : Alerte "Nouveau Ravitaillement" (Staff Watch)**
  "Smartwatch/Smartphone notification for protocol. Haptic feedback design. 'Table 5 calls for more Skol'. Quick response buttons: 'On my way', 'Busy'. Minimalist black background with white text."
- **Prompt 077 : Message de l'Organisateur au Staff**
  "Broadcast message screen. 'Everyone: Bride is entering. Stop service for 5 mins'. Large, clear text overlay. High attention design. Apple 'Emergency Alert' style but calmer."
- **Prompt 288 : Dashboard "Grace" - État Civil Event**
  "Specialized stats for a wedding. 'Bottles opened: 124'. 'Glasses served: 560'. Progress bars in soft orange. No currency, total dedication to service."

---

## 🍾 05. Validation Logistique (Stock Return)

- **Prompt 033 : Validation Serveur d'Évènement**
  "Streamlined validation for weddings. One-tap button: 'Serve Table X'. Summary shows: 'Total served to this group: 24 drinks'. Focus on logistics and speed, zero financial data visible to the server."
- **Prompt 079 : Aide au Protocole - Guide des Gestes**
  "Mini-manual for staff. 'How to serve Champagne properly?', 'Using the Badge-Scan for Events'. Cards with high-quality illustrations. Minimalist and helpful."
