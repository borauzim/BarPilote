# BarPilote : Design Prompts - Role 3 : Client de Bar (Jean / Consommateur) 🍏🥤🍎

**Style Global** : Apple "Consumer" Experience. Palette **Orange Vibrant**. Typographie SF Pro Rounded (plus chaleureuse). Focus sur la beauté des visuels produits, la rapidité de commande (Zéro friction) et l'innovation de la "Boucle".

---

## 📸 01. Accès au Menu & Scanning (Onboarding)

- **Prompt 041 : Splash Screen Client (PWA)**
  "Minimalist splash screen for BarPilote customer app. Pure white background. In center, a high-quality 3D render of a cold beer glass with condensation. Below, the 'BarPilote' logo in elegant SF Pro. Bottom label: 'Ready to fly. Please wait...'. Soft fade-in animation."
- **Prompt 241 : Arrivée Scan QR (Contextual)**
  "UX Flow Step 1: Client scans table QR. Instant splash screen with orange wing logo. Smooth transition to 'Welcome Table 4'. Soft white background with high-quality product categories in horizontal scroll."
- **Prompt 042 : Accueil Menu - Bienvenue au Lounge**
  "Main landing page after QR scan. Top header: 'Welcome to Lounge Pilote - Table 12'. Below, a scrolling horizontal bar of categories: 'Beers', 'Spirits', 'Softs', 'Wines'. Active category in bold blue. Minimalist search icon at top right. Aesthetic: Airy, spacious, premium."

## 🥤 02. Sélection de Boissons & Plateau (Ordering)

- **Prompt 043 : Grille de Produits (Menu Page)**
  "A grid of drink products for BarPilote. Each card: High-res square photo of the drink on a soft gray background. Name below (e.g., 'Heineken 33cl'), Price ('4,500 FC'). Top right of card: Small '+' icon in a circle. Clean, professional, Apple Store app vibe."
- **Prompt 213 : Fiche Produit Compacte (Menu Grid)**
  "Small square card with soft rounded corners. High-quality transparent drink photo. Name below. Small orange tag in top left: 'New!'. Action '+' button at bottom right."
- **Prompt 044 : Fiche Détail Produit - Bière**
  "Detail view for a specific beer. Large photo at top. Description: 'Une Heineken bien frappée, servie dans son verre givré'. Temperature indicator icon: 'Extra Cold'. Large 'Add to Tray' button at bottom. Style: Typography-driven, high contrast, elegant."

## 🔄 03. La Boucle de Commande (The Loop Innovation) 🚀

- **Prompt 049 : Proposition "Activer une Boucle"**
  "Overlay card after a successful order. 'Enjoying your drinks?'. 'Activate a Loop to get the same order automatically every 2 hours'. Minimalist toggle switch. Illustration: A circular arrow icon. Design follows Apple's 'Focus Mode' setup screens."
- **Prompt 050 : Configuration de la Récurrence (Client)**
  "Setup screen for a Recurring Loop. Slider to choose interval: 'Every 60, 90, 120, 150 minutes'. Summary: 'We will bring you 3 Turbos every 2 hours'. Action: 'Start My Loop'. Visual: Simple, intuitive, high-end."
- **Prompt 246 : Suivi du Premier Ravitaillement Automatique**
  "Customer dashboard. A countdown overlay: 'Loop Active: Round 2 in 12:45'. The circle filling with orange. Jean can see the status of the server: 'Tshala is on the way'. Clean Apple style."
- **Prompt 247 : Ajustement de la Boucle (On-the-go)**
  "Jean wants to slow down. Slider UI: 'Next delivery: +30 mins'. The timer updates instantly in orange. 'Round 2 scheduled for 22:30'. Professional, clear and empowering design."
